Using well-known APIs when building an app on React is crucial for several reasons. Firstly, established APIs are typically well-documented, offering comprehensive guides and examples that streamline integration, reducing development time and effort. This thorough documentation also aids in troubleshooting, making it easier to resolve issues quickly.

Secondly, well-known APIs are usually robust and reliable, having been tested extensively in various scenarios. They are maintained by experienced teams who regularly update the API to fix bugs, enhance security, and add new features, ensuring your app remains secure and up-to-date.
