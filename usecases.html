Jodacare and Jodapro are two products in daily use in Norwegian healthcare and police work

Jodacare

Our goal is to strengthen the dignity of people with dementia and other disabilities, and their relation to health personnel and their own families. By giving this group of vulnerable people a feeling of coherence in their day to day life through the use of pictures and dialog, we create a sense of calm for both service receivers and their families.

Jodacare

Jodabook

Jodabooks goal is to strengthen the dignity of people with different disabilities. Jodabook is their own app for daily use and communication.

Jodabook

Jodapro for prehospital care

For our paramedics, they get decision support from the right doctor at the right hospital to reduce misunderstandings and travel time.

Jodapro

Jodapro for homecare

To reduce travel for patients, engage homecare in more advanced care and share knowledge from hospital to municipalities, we use Jodapro in wound care.

Jodapro

Jodapro for fish industry

To reduce travel for veterinarians, technicians and expertice not located on site. Lerøy uses Jodapro for efficiency and fish health.

Jodapro

Jodapro for forensics

Forensic archaeology is a specialized field within forensic science that applies archaeological methodologies to locate, investigate, and recover various forms of concealed physical evidence, including human remains. For the police, they get decision support from the right forensic expert to reduce misunderstandings and travel time.
