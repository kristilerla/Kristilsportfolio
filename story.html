Jodacare and Jodapro are two products in daily use in Norway

Jodacare

Our goal is to strengthen the dignity of people with dementia and other disabilities, and their relation to health personnel and their own families. By giving this group of vulnerable people a feeling of coherence in their day-to-day life through the use of pictures and dialogue, we create a sense of calm for both service receivers and their families.

Jodabook

Jodabook's goal is to strengthen the dignity of people with different disabilities. Jodabook is their own app for daily use and communication.

Jodapro Wound Care

Homecare nurses perform advanced wound care at home with the specialist giving support from the hospital. This is a win-win situation for all, less money and time spent for patients, nurses, and specialists.

Jodapro App for Healthcare

Homecare nurses need support from their colleagues in a safe and easy way. The Jodapro app for phones does this securely and very easily! This is a win-win situation for all, less money and time spent for patients, nurses, and specialists. We use the Norwegian secure Health network in our app, which is built on Pexip and Webex.
