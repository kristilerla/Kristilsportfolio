Jodacare and Jodapro are two products in daily use in Norwegian healthcare

Jodacare

Our goal is to strengthen the dignity of people with dementia and other disabilities, and their relation to health personnel and their own families. By giving this group of vulnerable people a feeling of coherence in their day to day life through the use of pictures and dialog, we create a sense of calm for both service receivers and their families.

Jodapro

To reduce travel for patients, engage homecare in more advanced care and share knowledge from hospital to municipalities, we use Jodapro in wound care. For our paramedics, they get decision support from the right doctor at the right hospital to reduce misunderstandings and travel time.
